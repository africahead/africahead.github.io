Basic Idea

Moyom (MYM) is the utility currency of Ipparts Exchange (IPPAEX), a new cryptocurrency exchange in development, listing brand tokens. IPPAEX must fill the funding gap between the time good ideas originate and the time current funding happens. Current development theory has serious problems, relating to utilitarian imparting of ideas, explained with the philosophy Intequinism. MYM must pay for the purchasing of brand tokens. Brand tokens will capture intequity (capital of ideas) as soon as possible, after intequity formed. MYM can thus be compared to fiat, and brand tokens to shares, except, brand tokens, contractually, represent abstract assets, not physical assets, like shares.
1'000'000'000'000'000 (one quadrillion) MYM were minted. Around 500'000'000'000'000 (five hundred trillion) are being issued on app.ledgerdex.com, to fund the building of a know your client (KYC) exchange. The other 500 trillion are kept to buy brand tokens with in the future, to fund brand development.

LEDGERDEX: An OPPORTUNITY to GROW economies EXPONENTIALLY on Ethereum

App.LedgerDex.Com is an exchange where any ERC20 token can be traded for any other ERC20 token. If LedgerDex is used at its full potential, it can make a big contribution to the Crypto Community and economies in general. Usually, new economic projects are only funded by fiat currencies, ETH, BTC etc. New economic projects can however be funded with any liquid ERC20 token with value. Imagine what boosting effect it could have on economies, when more currencies start funding economic projects. Another benefit at LedgerDex, is, a maker can specify the taker, to limit an offer to a specific address. That means commissions on over the counter sales of ERC20 tokens can easily be administered, because sales transactions can be linked to specific addresses.

Ipparts Exchange makes use of the LedgerDex technology. Any liquid ERC20 token can be used to fund the development of IPPAEX, whilst buying brand tokens and MYM. Africahead however lists MYM only against USD stable and the brand tokens only against MYM. Therefore if someone wants to fund IPPAEX's development with other tokens they must contact Africahead to make arrangements. Offers to buy MYM and brand tokens, with any token, can be placed on LedgerDex, but it is not certain the offers will be seen, unless informed about it. After an over the counter sale for tokens, has been agreed upon, Africahead will sell the funding tokens on the market to convert it to fiat, ETH, BTC etc, to use for normal business expenses. The liquidity of such tokens, funding development, will be improved when Africahead sells the tokens for mainstream currencies. Anyone who introduces buyers for brand tokens and MYM will earn 3% of the amount.
