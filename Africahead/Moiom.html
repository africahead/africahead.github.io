Moiom (MIM) Utility Tokens

Moiom (MIM), ERC20 token is not currently in use. It was replaced with Moyom (MYM).

The Contract Address of MIM on the Ethereum Blockchain is:
0x3bEa1Cb0Ea10A35B199 5233D6d57B34Fb3142b1B.
When copying and pasting the address, remove the space between 9 and 5.

Moyom (MYM), an ERC20 token, with improved functionality, replacing MIM, was registered on the Ethereum Blockchain. The Contract Address of MYM on the blockchain is:
0xb9892f9a892f3e251fc5 8b9076c56add528eb8a6.
When applying the address, remove the space between 5 and 8.

Before interacting with MYM, users must make sure the correct Contract Address above, is used.

MYM can be purchased at decentralised cryptocurrency exchanges on the Internet. More information available at MYM's webpage.
