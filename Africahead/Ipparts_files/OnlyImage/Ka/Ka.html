Kaeuoi brand

This webpage contains old information. Further developments for the Kaeuoi brand, happens on the Kaeuoi (KAE) brand token issue webpage and the Kaeuoi website at Github.

The purpose of this page currently is to invite people to buy test Kaeuoi brand tokens, with a Metamask wallet or other Ethereum wallet at the FriendsFingers.com test crowdsale, with free test ETH available at the faucet on the Rinkeby Testnet. It is educational to do it and it is rather fun playing with fake ETH, buying brand tokens, isn't it?

The purpose is further to form awareness of a coming crowdsale to raise capital for Kaeuoi brand.
