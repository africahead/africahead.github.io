What is MYM?

Moyom (MYM) is venture capital / angel money, used on Ipparts Exchange (IPPAEX) (in development), to buy and sell brand currencies contractually backed by goodwill.

Goodwill is an abstract asset worth trillions, included in share prices, but often not capitalised in equity of companies managing it under International Accounting Standards. Goodwill exists at the start of business — so it can be used to raise startup capital in the form of brand currencies. Established businesses can also monetize their goodwill by listing brand currencies on IPPAEX.

1,000,000,000 MYM were issued on the Solana chain. MYM is traded at pump.fun and swap.pump.fun. MYM is also issued over the counter on the Ethereum Blockchain; the OTC issue price on Ethereum follows the Solana price.

Community Trading Policy

At pump.fun and swap.pump.fun, MYM is in a liquidity pool with Solana (SOL). A risk in the liquidity pool is large buyers dumping on small buyers. Therefore the community policy is: do not buy more than 20 million MYM. This reduces the risk of large buyers dumping on small buyers. Buying and holding 20 million MYM or less means a holder's value can grow with the project, whilst not scaring away other buyers.
