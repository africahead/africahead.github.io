Moyom (MYM) Utility Token on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC)

Download the .png MYM logo with transparent background or use this link for the logo.

Download the .svg MYM logo with transparent background or use this link for the logo.

NB: Developing MYM is a long term project. No guarantees can be given and it is not a token to buy for short term profit or retirement savings.

MYM is being issued for 125'000'000 USD value. MYM is issued over the counter on the Ethereum Blockchain and the Binance Smart Chain. MYM will preferably be issued in a paper contract, and later be converted, to the most effective form, after additional research, done with the new funds raised. Commission on placing the whole issue is 3%. That is equal to 3.75 million USD.

Moyom (MYM), an ERC20 token, was registered as BEP20 token on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC). The only approved Contract Address of MYM on BSC is:

0x711bd5c4a82c7be2db27026b0f7f10bb815c4b89

Before interaction with MYM on the Binance Smart Chain, users must make sure the correct Contract Address above, is used.

Short Term Roadmap

1) Sell Moyom (MYM) over the counter

2) Use some of the receipts to form a liquidity pool at Pancakeswap between USDC and MYM at the market price.
